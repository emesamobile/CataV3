Flor de Muga 2019
Back

Grapes harvested by hand at the peak of ripeness and transported to the winery in small crates and kept refrigerated in order to keep all their aromatic potential intact. After a short period of maceration in contact with the skins, 50% of the free-run must is chosen, the first juice to run out of the press simply by gravity.

Muga Reserva Selección Especial 2015
Back

After fermentation with indigenous yeasts in oak vats, the wine is Aged for 26 months, 40% in new french oak barrel and 60% in second-fill french barrel made in our cooperage. After being fined with fresh egg whites, the wine is bottled, after which it remains in our cellars for at least twelve months for further ageing

RODA I 2015
Back

Autumn and Winter with lot of rain so the cycle began with enough water on the ground. Very cold winter. Spring fresh at its start but it changed in May, which was really warm and made the phenology process progress (flowering 1st June). During May it did not rain at all, due to the winter reserves of water, it did not matter.
